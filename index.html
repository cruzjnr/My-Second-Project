My Favourite Footbal Club: Manchester United

While growing up, Manchester United was in their Utmost prime with the likes of the Christiano Ronaldo(GOAT), Wayne Rooney and the rest of our legends. That made me fall in love with the club.

But tragedy befell us in 2013 when our legend, pillar, father, Alex Ferguson left us. We won 13 premier League titles and other trophies with Alex Ferguson, but the moment he left, Manchester United fell apart. The greatest football club ever became a ridicle😔, but we never gave up.

We have had six managers since Alex Ferguson left but nothing still changed. But what kept us going was their resilience and zeal on the pitch. And when all hope was lost under Ruben Amorim, God gave us Carrick and our smile returned once more. oh what a wonderful couch! Carrick brought back the team to itself again and Bruuunoo Fernandez, the magician of the the club, brought back our lost glory to Man United.
